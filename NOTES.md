# Notes and TODOs

This site is a work-in-progress and will ultimately be the basis for my personal design system. In the mean time this is an incomplete playground where I can experiment with various APIs and naming conventions for the larger system. Some of this work will end up in [chance-ui](https://github.com/chaance/chance-ui).

This blog is over-engineered on purpose. Firstly, it's not intended to stay just a blog. Secondly, like I said above it's my playground and I treat it as a place to explore uninhibited. Feel free to shame me, but I built it how I wanted it and that's just the way it's gonna be.
