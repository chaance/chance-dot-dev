Hi! From time to time someone will ask me about a particular piece of gear, software, or some custom editor setting I use. Because I like jumping on trends, I created this page to answer those questions and keep a log of all my stuff.

## Workspace

![A work desk with a wooden top showing a audio/video production setup, with a microphone mounted on the desk, a closed dark gray laptop, black headphones, a dark gray keyboard and mouse, a widescreen monitor, and DSLR camera mounted above a stream deck controller, and bright video lighting above it all.](/workspace.jpg)

- **Desk:** [Uplift standing desk](https://www.upliftdesk.com/adjustable-height-desks/) with the [wooden top in acacia](https://www.upliftdesk.com/solid-wood-desktops-by-uplift-desk/).
- **Laptop:** Macbook Pro 15-inch, 2018. Thankfully I've never had any keyboard issues, but the battery has gotten pretty lousy. Otherwise she still does the job. I'm looking forward to a new machine after the MagSafe returns!
- **Desktop PC:** I run a dual setup between my laptop and [a PC I use for [very] occasional gaming and cross-platform testing](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07W8B2WP8/).
- **Monitor:** [Samsung 591 Series C27F591 27"](https://www.bhphotovideo.com/c/product/1237295-REG/samsung_591_series_c27f591_27.html/). This monitor is ok, decent quality but nothing to write home about. Looking to upgrade to something a little more HD at some point but it gets the job done.
- **Hubs and such:** The [CalDigit TS3 Plus](https://www.caldigit.com/ts3-plus/) is a solid little workstation with most of the ports I need, but I like to have a few extra USB ports so I have an [Anker Data Hub](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00VDVCQ84/) for backup.
- **Other:** Apple Magic Mouse 2 and Magic Keyboard with the numpad in space gray. [This fancy leather mousepad](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07CZR2RPY/) is nice.

Did you notice how I don't have a bunch of messy cables under my desk despite it being loaded with electronics? [That's all thanks to this video which tells you everything you need to know about getting the rat's nest in order.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tpk0GIpq5JM)

### Audio/Video

- **Camera+:** I use the Sony a6400 with the Sigma C 16mm lens. For mounting it to the desk and lighting, I basically stole [this guy's entire setup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WedG8LKO6ks), so watch that video and get the gear he recommends if you want this look.
- **Microphone:** [Shure SM7B](https://www.shure.com/en-US/products/microphones/sm7b) is my daily driver, though I also have the [Heil PR-40](https://heilsound.com/products/pr-40/) that desperately needs some electrical work. The mic mounts to my desk via the [Yellowtec Mika boom arm](https://www.yellowtec.com/mika.html).
- **Sound:** The mic runs into a [DBX 286s](https://dbxpro.com/en/products/286s), into the [Focusrite Scarlett 18i20](https://focusrite.com/en/usb-audio-interface/scarlett/scarlett-18i20) which gets the signal to my computer. This gear is tucked away nicely in a Gator rack mount that also houses a large pull-out drawer and a [Furman M-8X2](https://www.furmanpower.com/product/15a-standard-power-conditioner/) to power it all.
- **Other:** The camera connects to my computer for streaming and webcam use via the [Elgato Cam Link 4K](https://www.elgato.com/en/gaming/cam-link-4k). I use the [Elgato Stream Deck](https://www.elgato.com/en/gaming/stream-deck) for programming different scenes and shortcuts while I'm on camera. I've got these nice [white soundproofing panels](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07R96B7CZ/) hanging on the wall.
